Siri Shortcuts Setup

📱 How to use with Siri:

1. iPhone Shortcuts App:

  • Open Shortcuts app
  • Tap "+" to create new shortcut
  • Add action: "Get Contents of URL"
  • URL: https://yourapi.com/api/tasks?status=pending,failed
  • Add action: "Ask for" → Text with prompt "Retry task ID?"
  • Add action: "Get Contents of URL" (POST)
  • URL: https://yourapi.com/api/tasks/{provided text}/retry
  • Headers: Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY

2. Voice Command:

  • Tap the ⓘ info button on your shortcut
  • Enable "Ask Siri"
  • Set phrase: "Recover tasks" or "Retry last error"

3. Common Voice Commands:

  • "Hey Siri, recover tasks" — lists incomplete tasks
  • "Hey Siri, retry task" — prompts for task ID and retries
  • "Hey Siri, escalate error" — escalates to operator
recoveryOS Help

What is recoveryOS?

recoveryOS is an error recovery orchestration platform that:

  • Detects errors from your systems (errorOS)
  • Categorizes & analyzes root causes
  • Queues recovery tasks (errin-parser)
  • Retries intelligently with exponential backoff
  • Escalates to operators when needed
  • Integrates with Siri for voice control

Task Statuses:

  • pending — waiting to be processed
  • in_progress — worker is attempting recovery
  • completed — successfully recovered
  • failed — attempt failed; will retry
  • escalated — exceeded max attempts; needs operator
