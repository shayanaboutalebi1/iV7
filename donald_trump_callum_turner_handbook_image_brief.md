# Image Brief

## Requested scene
Create an editorial-style image of **Donald Trump** holding a book titled:

**"Callum Turner Self-Representation: International Law Handbook"**

## Composition
- Medium shot, subject centered.
- Book cover clearly visible and legible.
- Neutral background (office or library tone).
- Natural lighting, realistic proportions.

## Style options
1. Photorealistic editorial photo style.
2. Digital illustration style (clean, high contrast).

## Safety / quality constraints
- No logos or trademarks unless licensed.
- No misleading claims presented as factual endorsements.
- Keep typography on the book cover readable.

## Output specs
- Aspect ratio: 4:5 and 16:9 variants.
- Minimum resolution: 2048px on the long edge.
- Export: PNG.
